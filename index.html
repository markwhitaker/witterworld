Every film we’ve watched so far...

I created this site in May 2020 to track a new feature on the Kermode and Mayo's Film Review show on BBC Radio 5 Live (known to its fans as Wittertainment).

A couple had emailed the show from New Zealand on 24 April (listen here at 32:00), saying they were aiming to watch a film from every country in the world and asking listeners for ideas from their home countries.

A couple of weeks later, on 15 May, this site was announced to the world and gave the feature its name! (Listen on SoundCloud or here at 25:55.)

The feature ended on Friday 31 July.
